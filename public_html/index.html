Tell the heroic story nobody else could. All the scaffolding and structure of a board game. All the freedom of imagination.

A Thousand Faces of Adventure is a story-generating board game. Gather at a table with your friends and it will guide your creation of an improvisational story. Play as the GM, with narrative authority over the world and events, or play as a heroic character exploring this new world and meeting its challenges!


The players will build their characters from scrappy adventurers into world-changing heroes, while the GM earns points when the story drums each beat of The Hero's Journey.

A Role-Playing Game That's a Board Game

A structure that sets you free. A Thousand Faces of Adventure gives you the familiar physicality of board game components -- cards, decks, tokens -- so your table can focus on the story. Gloomhaven meets Blades in the Dark. Sleeping Gods meets Ironsworn.

A Board Game That's a Role-Playing Game

Are you a fan of RPGs and your friends are all board gamers? Looking for the right RPG to introduce them to the world of play-to-find-out-what-happens? A Thousand Faces of Adventure was designed for you.

No 200-page tome. No wrong answers. When new players sit down to play, they see familiar board game components, not a wall of text. The GM walks everyone through a well-tested 45-minute setup that builds the characters and your world together, from scratch.

All the math is baked-in. The probability curves are carefully designed and distributed into a 20-card deck, so players only need to flip a card and see the result on the face.
